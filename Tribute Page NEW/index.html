Francis N - UFC Heavyweight champion

Francis Ngannou's life and education

  • Born on the 5th September 1986 Batie-Cameroon.
  • 1996 - Worked in a sand quarry in Batie because of lack of school fees aged just 10.
  • 2008 - Started training in boxing.
  • 2012 - Decided to leave Cameroon to head to Paris, France; where he met Fernand Lopez who trained him in the MMA career

Fighting Championship of Francis Ngannou

  1. November 2013 - Started his MMA career in the french promotion 100% fight and compiled a record of 5 - 1 before heading to the UFC.
  2. 19 December 2015 - Started in the UFC and fought via knockout Luis Henrique in the second round.
  3. From 10 April 2016 to 28 March 2020 - Francis Ngannou has won 8 games via knockout and TKO
  4. 27 March 2021 - Francis Ngannou earned the "Performance of the night" via knockout in the second round with Miocic.
  5. 22 January 2022 - Francis Ngannou won the UFC Heavyweight Championship against Ciryl Gane.
